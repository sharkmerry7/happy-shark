Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our portable miniature golf rentals

How much space is needed for your 5-hole mini golf course? +

Our 5-hole course requires approximately 500-600 square feet of space (roughly 20' x 30'). Each hole takes about 100 square feet, plus we need some space for movement between holes. We can adapt the layout to your venue's specific dimensions, so if you're concerned about space, let us know and we can discuss options!

How long does it take to set up and tear down? +

Setup typically takes about 60-90 minutes, and teardown takes approximately 45-60 minutes. We recommend booking us for a minimum of 3 hours for your event, which includes setup and teardown time. We always arrive at least 2 hours before your event starts to ensure everything is perfectly ready when your guests arrive.

Can the mini golf course be set up indoors? +

Absolutely! Our course can be set up both indoors and outdoors. For indoor setups, we need a flat surface with adequate ceiling height (minimum 7 feet). Many clients choose indoor setups for corporate events, winter celebrations, or when weather is a concern. Our course is designed to be floor-friendly and won't damage your venue's flooring.

What is your service area? +

We serve events within a 50-mile radius of the downtown area. This includes all major suburbs and neighboring communities. For locations beyond this radius, we may be able to accommodate with an additional travel fee. Please contact us with your specific location to confirm availability.

How many people can play on the course at once? +

Our 5-hole course can comfortably accommodate 15-20 players simultaneously (3-4 players per hole). For larger events, guests naturally rotate through the course, creating a fun, social atmosphere. Our typical capacity allows for 60-80 people to enjoy the course within a 2-hour event. For larger events, we recommend booking for a longer duration to ensure everyone gets a chance to play.

What is included in your rental package? +

Our standard package includes:

  • 5 professionally designed mini golf holes
  • Putters in various sizes (adult and child sizes)
  • Golf balls in a variety of colors
  • Scorecards and pencils
  • Setup and teardown
  • On-site attendant for the duration of your event
  • Customizable hole flags (optional add-on for weddings and corporate events)
Do you require a deposit to book? +

Yes, we require a 50% deposit to secure your event date, with the remaining balance due one week before your event. We accept all major credit cards, checks (with advance notice), and digital payment options. For corporate events, we can provide invoicing options as needed.

What is your cancellation policy? +

Our cancellation policy is as follows:

  • 30+ days before event: Full refund of deposit
  • 14-29 days before event: 50% refund of deposit
  • Less than 14 days before event: Deposit is non-refundable

For weather-related cancellations of outdoor events, we offer flexible rescheduling options or can move to an indoor location if available.
